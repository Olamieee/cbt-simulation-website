WELCOME TO THE PROTOTYPE OF THE CBT SIMULATOR FOR WEST AFRICAN SENIOR SCHOOL CERTIFICATE EXAMINATION (WASSCE)

This platform is part of a pioneering project, funded by TetFund and designed by Dr. Faloye Bankole Olagunjy (PhD) at Bamidele Olumilua University of Education, Science, and Technology, Ikere-Ekiti, Nigeria. The objective of this Institution-Based Research (IBR) project is to create a simulation tool that prepares senior secondary students for the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE) through an advanced Computer-Based Test (CBT) experience.

This innovation aims to enhance accessibility and ease of examination preparation, particularly for students across West Africa. By leveraging innovative CBT technology, students can gain familiarity with the examination format, improve their test-taking skills, and build confidence as the examination approaches. In a nutshell, this project intends to manage CBT phobia for students in the area of Oral English and Reading Comprehension.

Thank you for visiting, and we hope this platform becomes a valuable tool towards charting a veritable course in achieving success in CBT in the WASSCE.
