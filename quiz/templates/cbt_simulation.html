Welcome to WASSCE CBT Simulation

This platform provides a simulation of the WASSCE CBT exam to help you practice under real-time conditions. Choose the type of test you want to take: either the Oral Test or the Reading Comprehension Test.

Follow the instructions on each test page to begin. Once you start, you will have a limited amount of time to answer all the questions. Make sure to answer every question before submitting.

This test simulates the WASSCE Oral English section. It includes questions on pronunciation, stress patterns, and intonation.
